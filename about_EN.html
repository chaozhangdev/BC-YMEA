About us

The BC YOUNG MINDS EDUCATION ASSOCIATION is a non-profit organization registered and approved by the government. Located in Surrey, the association targets and serves young people throughout the Greater Vancouver region, helping immigrant families to better understand and integrate Canadian educational resources, service resources, and social resources to better develop new life in Canada.

Objective: Let more young people have the opportunity to share quality education resources and education services; let more parents get rid of the confusion and troubles of educating their children! All activities are based on non-profit-making services.

Mission: Respect the growth characteristics of young people, integrate high-quality educational resources and parents' interaction and cooperation, scientifically plan and help young people to quickly adapt to Canada's learning and growth environment, and strive to make young people grow up: love others, self-discipline, optimism, and benefit Group, quality and excellent prospective citizens!

The association has a group of members with similar ideas. Through interaction and cooperation with parents, it has launched a series of lectures and activities that are beneficial to the healthy growth and academic progress of young people. Services include: daily integration learning, accompanying companionship, parent-child relationship, family education, further education, technology exploration, cultural entertainment, physical fitness, experience exchange, short-term, long-term follow-up services, regular seminars and club activities on different topics, leading Immigrant students explore Canadian history, science, culture, landscapes and customs, showcase talent, serve the community, and engage in daily interactive counseling.
