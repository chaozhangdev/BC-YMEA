Membership Application

Members of the BC Youth Education Association (hereinafter referred to as the "Association") are individual members. Individual members include adult members and student members. The membership period is one year.

Adult Membership Conditions:
  1. Have the will to join the association and abide by the rules of the association.
  2. Enthusiastic youth education, with no clear record of criminal history.
  3. Actively support the work of the Association and be willing to participate in volunteer work or engage in various activities and management of the Association.
Membership Requirements for Student Membership:
  1. Have the will to join the association and abide by the rules of the association.
  2. K-G12 students are willing to participate in the activities of the Association.
  3. Student members are applied by parents on their behalf.
Admission Procedure:
  1. Fill in the personal information of the applicant
  2. Become a member of the association by signing up for the association
